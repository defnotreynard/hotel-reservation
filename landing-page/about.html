ABOUT

Hotel Cebu is a 4-star hospitality property located in Mandaue City, Cebu. Opened last 2017, bai Hotel Cebu is an independent hotel. Nestled between the bustling cities of Mandaue and Cebu, it presents spectacular harbor and city views with ultra-modern amenities and facilities. A contemporary chic business hotel and culinary destination in one of the country’s top business and leisure destinations.  All 668 guestrooms and 8 restaurants are stylishly designed in contemporary hues that evoke an ambiance of modern luxury and spaciousness secured with a world-class access control system.
